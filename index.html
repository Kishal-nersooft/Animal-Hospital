Welcome

Welcome to Best Care Animal Hospital

Best Care Animal Hospital is dedicated to offering you quality veterinary service at a reasonable price. We are capable of a vast range of services — from vaccination and treatments to surgeries, digital X-ray, pet taxi, and ambulance services. We are dedicated to providing the best care for your pet companions throughout their lives.

  • Vaccinations & general health check-ups
  • Modern surgery unit with gaseous anesthesia
  • Inward care, boarding & 24h dog handlers
  • Mobile veterinary & field surgery units
  • Pet shop and pharmacy on premises
  • Pet taxi & ambulance services
